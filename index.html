Welcome to Royal Family

Royal Enfield is a renowned motorcycle manufacturer known for its classic and vintage-styled bikes. Originally a British company, Royal Enfield is now based in India and is a subsidiary of Eicher Motors. It has a rich history dating back to 1901, making it one of the oldest motorcycle brands in continuous production. Royal Enfield bikes are known for their iconic design, distinctive thumping engine sound, and robust build. Popular models include the Bullet, Classic, Himalayan, and Meteor, among others. The brand has a loyal fan base and is celebrated for its blend of retro aesthetics and modern performance, making it a popular choice for riders who appreciate the charm of classic motorcycles



About

Royal Enfield is one of the oldest and most iconic motorcycle brands in the world, known for its timeless design, rugged performance, and rich heritage. Here's a more detailed overview:

History and Origins

  • Founding:

    Royal Enfield started in 1901 in Redditch, England, originally as a manufacturer of bicycles and later as a producer of motorcycles. The brand's motto was "Made like a gun," reflecting the company's origins in armaments and its reputation for reliability and durability

  • Indian Connection:

    In 1955, Royal Enfield partnered with Madras Motors in India to assemble the Bullet 350, a model that became synonymous with the brand. In 1971, the British company ceased operations, but production continued in India. Eventually, Royal Enfield became a wholly Indian company, now owned by Eicher Motors. Popular Models

    • Popular Models

  • Bullet:

    The Royal Enfield Bullet is one of the longest-running production motorcycles in the world, known for its classic design and iconic thumping sound.



  • Classic 350 & 500:

    These models blend vintage looks with modern technology, maintaining the essence of Royal Enfield's retro appeal while incorporating updated engineering.



  • Himalayan:

    Launched in 2016, the Himalayan is an adventure touring motorcycle designed for off-road and on-road riding, catering to the growing market of adventure bikers.



  • Interceptor 650 & Continental GT 650:

    These are modern twin-cylinder motorcycles introduced in 2018, offering a mix of retro style and modern performance, and have been well-received globally.



    • Design and Engineering

    Royal Enfield motorcycles are celebrated for their classic styling, which includes rounded headlamps, spoked wheels, and a retro silhouette. Despite their vintage appeal, the motorcycles have evolved with modern features such as fuel injection, disc brakes, ABS, and more efficient engines.

    Global Expansion

    Over the years, Royal Enfield has expanded its market globally, establishing itself in North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia. It has positioned itself as a brand that offers an accessible entry into the world of classic motorcycling.

    Services

    Some dealerships offer customization services to personalize motorcycles with genuine accessories, color options, and more.

    • Authorized Service Centers

  • Routine Maintenance:

    These centers provide regular servicing, repairs, and maintenance using genuine Royal Enfield parts and trained technicians.

  • Extended Warranties and Service Packages:

    Options like extended warranties and service packages (such as Annual Maintenance Contracts) are often available, providing cost-effective maintenance solutions.

    • Royal Enfield App

    The official app offers features like booking test rides, scheduling service appointments, tracking service history, and finding nearby dealerships or service centers. It also provides updates on events, rides, and new products
